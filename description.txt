UI Customizer!
Move the hotbar, chat, map, health, mana, and buff positions with ease! This mod allows you to move a significant amount of UI to your liking.

How to use:
Open up your inventory and press "UI Editor" in the bottom right corner.

Features:
- Editor: Allows you to move and customize all UI. Save and share layouts with your friends!
- Toggle Interface Layers: Toggle interface layers for each mod.
- Toggle Resource Packs: Easily enable and disable resource packs while in a world without exiting!
